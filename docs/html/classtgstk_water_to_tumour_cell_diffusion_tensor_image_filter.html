Detailed Description

Tumour cell diffusion tensor field computation from a DTI-derived water diffusion tensor field.

tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter computes a tumour cell diffusion tensor field \(\bar{\bar{D}}(\bar{r})\) from the water diffusion tensor field \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{water}(\bar{r})\) assessed by DTI as follows:

\[ \bar{\bar{D}}(\bar{r}) = \begin{cases}\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{white}(\bar{r}) \quad &\text{if } \bar{r} \in \Omega_\text{white} \bigcup \Omega_\text{oedema} \bigcup \Omega_\text{enhancing} \bigcup \Omega_\text{necrotic} \\ \bar{\bar{D}}_\text{grey} \quad &\text{if } \bar{r} \in \Omega_\text{grey} \\ \bar{\bar{0}} \quad &\text{otherwise} \end{cases} \]

where \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{white}(\bar{r})\) is the tumour diffusion tensor field in white matter; \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{grey}\) is the constant tumour diffusion tensor in grey matter; \(\bar{\bar{0}}\) is the null tensor; and \(\Omega_\text{white}\), \(\Omega_\text{oedema}\), \(\Omega_\text{enhancing}\), \(\Omega_\text{necrotic}\), and \(\Omega_\text{grey}\) are respectively the white matter, oedema, enhancing core, necrotic core, and grey matter subdomains.

The anisotropic tumour diffusion tensor field in white matter \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{white}\) is built as proposed in [2] :

\[ \bar{\bar{D}}_\text{white} = \frac{3 \, d_\text{white}} {\sum_{i=1}^3 \tilde{\lambda}_i(a)} \sum_{i=1}^3 \tilde{\lambda}_i(a) \, \bar{e}_i \, \bar{e}_i^\top \]

where \(d_\text{white}\) is the tumour cell mean diffusivity in white matter; \(\bar{e}_i\) is the \(i^\text{th}\) eigenvector of the water diffusion tensor \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{water}\); and \(\tilde{\lambda}_i(a) = l_i(a)\lambda_i\) with:

\[ \begin{bmatrix} l_1(a) \\ l_2(a) \\ l_3(a) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} a & a & 1 \\ 1 & a & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} c_l \\ c_p \\ c_s \end{bmatrix} \]

where \(a\) is the anisotropy factor and:

\[ c_l = \frac{\lambda_1-\lambda_2}{\lambda_1+\lambda_2+\lambda_3}, \quad c_p = \frac{2 \, (\lambda_2-\lambda_3)}{\lambda_1+\lambda_2+\lambda_3}, \quad c_s = \frac{3 \, \lambda_3}{\lambda_1+\lambda_2+\lambda_3} \]

\(\lambda_i\) being the \(i^\text{th}\) eigenvalue of the water diffusion tensor \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{water}\).

The isotropic constant tumour diffusion tensor in grey matter \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{grey}\) is given by:

\[ \bar{\bar{D}}_\text{grey} = \begin{bmatrix} d_\text{grey} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & d_\text{grey} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & d_\text{grey} \end{bmatrix} \]

where \(d_\text{grey}\) is the tumour cell mean diffusivity in grey matter.

The water diffusion tensor field \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{water} (\bar{r})\) and the brain map defining the domain \(\Omega\) can be specified by the user as vtkImageData objects. The white and grey matter tumour cell mean diffusivites \(d_\text{white}\) and \(d_\text{grey}\) can also be specified. Alternatively, an isotropic constant tumour diffusion tensor can be used for white matter as well.

◆ setTensorMode()

void tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter::setTensorMode ( TensorMode  mode)

Sets the diffusion tensor mode.

tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter::ANISOTROPIC will produce an anisotropic tumour cell diffusion tensor field in white matter \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{white}(\bar{r})\) derived from a user-provided water diffusion tensor field \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{water}(\bar{r})\) assessed by DTI using the method proposed in [2]. tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter::ISOTROPIC will produce a constant isotropic tumour cell diffusion tensor in white matter of mean diffusivity \(d_\text{white}\). No water diffusion tensor field must be provided for this mode.

◆ setWaterDiffusionTensorImage()

void tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter::setWaterDiffusionTensorImage ( vtkSmartPointer< vtkImageData >  image)

Sets the water diffusion tensor image \(\bar{\bar{D}}_\text{water}(\bar{r})\) assessed by DTI in \(\text{mm}^2\text{ d}^{-1}\).

The image scalar type must be VTK_DOUBLE. The image must have 6 ( \(\!D_{xx}, D_{xy}, D_{xz}, D_{yy}, D_{yz}, D_{zz}\) – implicitly symmetric) or 9 ( \(\!D_{xx}, D_{xy}, D_{xz}, D_{yx}, D_{yy}, D_{yz}, D_{zx}, D_{zy}, D_{zz}\)) scalar components. This image is required and used only if tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter::tensorMode is tgstkWaterToTumourCellDiffusionTensorImageFilter::ANISOTROPIC.
